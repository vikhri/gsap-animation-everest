About Nepalese Mountains

Discover the breathtaking landscapes and serene beauty of Nepal's mountains. From the awe-inspiring peaks of the Himalayas to lush valleys, Nepal offers an unforgettable experience for adventurers and nature enthusiasts

Mount Everest

Height:
8,848 meters (29,029 feet)
Difficulty:
Very challenging
Description:
Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world, offers an extreme challenge to climbers with its treacherous terrain and high altitude. Only experienced mountaineers attempt this formidable ascent.

Annapurna Range

Height:
Various peaks above 7,000 meters (22,966 feet)
Difficulty:
Moderate to difficult
Description:
The Annapurna Range provides a mix of moderate to challenging treks suitable for adventurers seeking diverse landscapes and cultural experiences. Treks in this region offer stunning views and cultural immersion.

Langtang Valley

Height:
Peaks around 7,000 meters (22,966 feet)
Difficulty:
Moderate
Description:
The Langtang Valley trek offers a moderate yet breathtaking journey through picturesque landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and encounters with local Tamang and Sherpa communities.
